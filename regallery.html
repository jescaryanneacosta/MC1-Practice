3. AT THE CROSSROADS

This is a gallery containing art by Yuumei. This particular piece is called "At the Crossroads". According to her, she was inspired by Makoto SHinkai's paintover works. This is actuallly a paintover of an actual crossroad photo taken in Japan. You can visit yuumei.com if you're interested in her other works.
